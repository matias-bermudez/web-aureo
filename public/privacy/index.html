Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Aureo (the "App"), developed by Matías Bermúdez, handles your information.

Summary

Aureo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter in the App stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or advertising tools
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All financial data you enter in Aureo (transactions, accounts, budgets) is stored locally on your device. Aureo does not have a backend, user accounts, or cloud sync.

Camera and Photo Library

Aureo requests access to the camera solely for receipt scanning. Receipt images are processed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework and are never uploaded to any server.

Aureo requests access to the photo library solely to attach receipt images to transactions. These images are never uploaded or shared.

In-App Purchases

Aureo offers optional paid subscriptions and a one-time purchase through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payments are processed entirely by Apple. Aureo does not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Aureo relies on Apple's In-App Purchase system for processing subscriptions and purchases. Apple may collect information according to its own privacy practices. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all information at any time by uninstalling the App or using the data reset options available within the App.

Children's Privacy

Aureo is not directed to children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
